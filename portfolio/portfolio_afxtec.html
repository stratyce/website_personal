AFX Technicality Logo

The first design project I did for a larger audience, the Technicality logo was a first in many aspects. Technicality was a dance team that I was part of, consisting of around 50 people. It was the first time I ever designed a logo that would become part of a tangible product for a large amount of people. It was also the first time for me to experiment with Adobe Illustrator, having received it from my university.

To rise up to the challenge, I took a crash course in Illustrator, graphic design, and typography from dozens of YouTube videos and online courses. It pushed my ability to learn, and learn quickly. It drove myself to expand my knowledge into fields I had limited experience but large interest in. The result was a few more skills under my belt, and a design that was loved by team members and non-team members alike.
