Stella's Child - Website

Stella's Child is a NGO that works mainly in Indonesia to bring career-centered education to those with less opportunities. I was able to work alongside their CEO, Tim Cameron, while designing and developing this site.

This project not only helped me contribute as a volunteer towards a greater cause, it also taught me about how to communicate with employers and deliver on a product that exceeds their vision. While working with some guidelines provided by Tim (color scheme, site interface, and general site layout), I used my own design principles to transform his suggestions into a functional yet elegant website. It just goes to show what cool things can be created when multiple people put their heads together.
